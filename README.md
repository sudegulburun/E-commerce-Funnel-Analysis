## Executive Summary

This project analyzes the customer journey in an e-commerce platform using funnel analysis.  
The goal is to understand how users move through key stages of the purchase process and identify where the biggest drop-offs occur.

The analysis focuses on three main funnel steps:

- Page View
- Add to Cart
- Begin Checkout

By measuring conversion rates between these stages, the project identifies optimization opportunities to improve the overall purchase conversion rate.

---

## Business Problem

E-commerce businesses often struggle with understanding where customers abandon the purchase journey.

Even when traffic is high, many users drop off before completing the checkout process.

Key business questions include:

- Where do users drop off in the purchase funnel?
- Which devices convert better?
- Which traffic sources drive higher conversions?
- What stage of the funnel needs optimization?

Understanding these patterns helps businesses improve conversion rates and maximize revenue.

---

## Methodology

The analysis was conducted using **MySQL** queries on an e-commerce dataset.

Steps followed:

1. Count users at each stage of the funnel
2. Calculate overall funnel conversion rate
3. Analyze conversion rate by device type
4. Analyze conversion rate by traffic source
5. Measure drop-off rates between funnel stages

Key metrics analyzed:

- Funnel Conversion Rate
- View-to-Cart Rate
- Cart-to-Checkout Rate
- Device Conversion Rate
- Traffic Source Conversion Rate

## Results & Business Recommendations

Insights: 

- Approximately 81% of users drop off before reaching checkout, indicating significant friction in the purchase journey.
- Mobile users show the highest conversion rate, suggesting that the mobile experience is relatively strong.
- Email marketing and paid search campaigns generate the highest conversion rates, indicating strong purchase intent.
- More than 50% of users leave after viewing a product without adding it to the cart, indicating potential product page optimization issues.

Recommendations: 

- Simplify the checkout process
- Improve page speed and layout for desktop users
- Increase investment in email marketing campaigns
- Improve product page information and visuals
